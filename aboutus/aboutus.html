About Us

Every garment manufacturer will experience defects in end products.
If there are such defects, they will incur major setbacks financially
due to FOB losses. It is one of the main problems that is encountered
by the garment industrialists. We are specialists in providing solutions
to the color defects without compensating the quality of the product.

OUR SERVICES

We can confidently give you solutions for the below problems which
are commonly faced in the color shading industry in garments:

  • - Color Shading
  • - Tone Changes
  • - Uneven shades
  • - Crease marks
  • - Remove the dye patches
  • - White spots
