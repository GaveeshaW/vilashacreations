Our Services

We Offer A Range Of Services
To Meet Your Needs

COLOR SHADING

We use raw materials to change
the colors which are different
between panels in the garments.
We can proudly say that we achieve
99.9% accuracy when it comes to
this service.

TONE CHANGES

We can change the tones between
panels in garments without
compensating the quality of the
product. Contact us for more details
and we are confident that we can
do a good job for you!

UNEVEN SHADES

With a focus on detail, we meticulously
restore uniformity in shades across all
panels in the garment. Clients can
trust in our dedication to delivering
flawlessly consistent garments.

CREASE MARKS

We utilize advanced techniques to
eliminate crease marks from
garments, ensuring a seamless
finish across all panels. Our
commitment to quality ensures
consistently flawless results.

REMOVE DYE PATCHES

We implement cutting-edge
techniques to remove dye patches
from garments ensuring a consistent
coloration throughout. We guarantee
impeccably restored garments free
from discolorations and dye patches.

WHITE SPOTS

Our dedication to quality extends to
the removal of white spots where
precision and attention to detail are
paramount. Utilizing specialized
methods, we ensure a pristine finish
on every garment.
